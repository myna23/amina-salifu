A Bit About Me

I am a computer engineer and data scientist with a background in mathematical sciences and artificial intelligence. My work focuses on speech technologies for non-native and Ghanaian English accents, communication-efficient IoT systems, and inclusive AI. I enjoy building models and tools that are robust, practical, and fair for people in low-resource settings.
